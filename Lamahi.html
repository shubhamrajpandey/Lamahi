Discover Lamahi

Lamahi is a small but growing town in the Dang district of western Nepal. It is known for its rich culture, friendly people, and weekly local markets. Surrounded by natural lakes, old temples, and peaceful villages, Lamahi is a great place to visit if you want to see the simple and beautiful way of life in this part of Nepal.
